Story

The Awakening

5,555 babies awaken in a lifeless laboratory - confused, but not quite abandoned.

A door in one wall opens up, and awaiting outside is paradise. A tropical biosphere is connected to the lab, and everything the Babies need is present. Gentle robots wheel around helping to feed the children fresh fruits and vegetables, keep the Babies clean and attend to their needs, and with whatever else they may need.

At the end of the day, the Babies are escorted to their rooms where they can sleep safely in their own beds - locked in, of course.

The Twist

Life continues for the Babies in their small utopia - a warm, safe environment filled where the days are filled with playtime, fun and hardly any rules. For most, it’s an ideal life. But as time goes by, some of the more adventurous and curious kids begin to question their state of being.

In time, a small group of the Babies venture as far as they can in this room, until they find a trip-switch hidden in a wall. A door opens to a dark hallway, and the Babies enter.

Several meters in, they encounter a fork in the hallway and a robot nanny standing guard. The robot speaks to them, presenting their options.

“Each of you now must make a decision. There are two paths you can take from here, the blue door, or the red door.

Pass through the blue door, and you’ll wake up tomorrow with no memories of this hallway, or decision today.

Go through the red door, and you’ll discover the unknown. If you seek the answers, to who and what you are, why you are here, and anything else, you can find them. But, you’ll never be able to return to the room again, no matter what kind of world exists beyond this safe haven.”

In time, the curious will need to make a decision. All of “The Babies” will earn credits while “staked” in the Biosphere, which can be used in the outside world to evolve, mature, breed and/or transcend.


When the time comes, what will you decide ?
